The Program Tracks
01
Mindset & Transition
The Pain Principle

A 5-stage framework for navigating change, transition, and the pivot. Built from almost 30 years of real experience. The foundation of every MiRAjO program. Available as a keynote, workshop, or self-paced course.

02
Entrepreneur Launch
90 Day Side Hustle

The flagship business launch program. Delivered in-person, online, or as a hybrid cohort. Covers business validation, operations, marketing, and financial setup. Designed for aspiring and early-stage entrepreneurs.

03
Financial Literacy
Personal & Business Finance

Two tracks in one. Personal financial literacy covers budgeting, habits, debt, and savings. Business financial literacy covers cash flow, profit and loss, pricing, and growth finance. Includes access to the Money Moves budgeting tool.

04
Leadership Development
LeadLoud

Based on the published book LeadLoud: 11 Commandments of the Next-Gen Leader. A practical leadership framework for emerging leaders, managers, and entrepreneurs who lead teams.

05
Team & Behavioral Assessment
DrivAmor

A proprietary behavioral assessment that identifies how individuals are wired to lead (Drive Mode) and relate (Amor Code). 20 combined profiles. Used for team development, conflict reduction, and leadership coaching.

06
Partnership & Co-Founders
Couples in Entrepreneurship

A program for couples and co-founders building a business together. Covers communication, role definition, financial alignment, and conflict resolution in a business partnership context.
