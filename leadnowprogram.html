About the Program

Leadership built for Tampa Bay's next generation of impact makers.

The Lead Now Program is a hands-on, immersive leadership experience designed for professionals ready to move beyond theory and into real impact. Each month includes one in-person workshop and a leadership field trip to a Tampa Bay business, because real leadership is learned in the room and in the field, not just on paper.

This program is about connection at the highest level. You will be surrounded by Tampa Bay's most driven professionals, building relationships that take your business to the next level. Facilitated by Miranda Johnson (CEO, MiRAjO Collective) and Roberto Borrero (CEO, Tampa Business Club), Tampa Bay's #1 networking group.

Monthly Curriculum

Six months. Six themes. One transformation.

Month 01 — January

Leadership Foundations

Establishing your leadership baseline, personal values, and the core principles that will guide your journey through the program.

Month 02 — February

Innovation & Technology

Exploring how technology is reshaping leadership. Learning to lead adaptive teams in rapidly changing environments.

Month 03 — March

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Building inclusive workplaces and understanding how diversity drives innovation and stronger business outcomes.

Month 04 — April

Spring Leadership Retreat

A two-day off-site retreat focused on vision, reflection, and deep collaboration with your cohort and facilitators.

Month 05 — May

Mental Wellness in Leadership

Understanding the critical role mental health plays in sustainable leadership and building resilience under pressure.

Month 06 — June

Future-Proofing Your Leadership

Finalizing your Community Impact Initiative and presenting at the graduation ceremony. Leaving with a clear leadership vision.
