LONG DESCRIPTION
----------------
Money Moves is not a spreadsheet. It is a visual budget calendar that puts every dollar on the day it moves — so you can see your money the way you actually live it.

Most budget apps give you lists and pie charts after the fact. Money Moves shows you what is coming, what already hit, and what your balance looks like every single day of the month — all on one calendar you can read at a glance.

See your money move.
Add income, expenses, and transfers directly to the calendar. Every transaction lands on its date. Your running daily balance updates automatically so you always know where you stand — not just at the end of the month, but on Tuesday the 14th when that bill hits.

Plan 7 years out.
Switch to Forecast view and see a month-by-month projection of your cash flow for the next 7 years — built entirely from your recurring transactions. Spot the months where money gets tight before they arrive. Make decisions based on where you are actually headed, not just where you are right now.

Built-in calculators — no app switching needed.
Mortgage Calculator — enter your home price, down payment, rate, and term. Get your full monthly payment broken down into principal, interest, taxes, insurance, and PMI. See lifetime costs and how much extra payments save you.
DTI Calculator — know your debt-to-income ratio before you apply for anything. Front-end and back-end ratios, color-coded so you can see exactly where you stand with lenders.
Debt Payoff Calculator — enter any debt, see your payoff date, total interest paid, and exactly how much you save by adding even $50 extra per month.
Floating Calculator — quick math without leaving your dashboard.

Multiple accounts, one picture.
Add checking, savings, credit cards, or any account you track. Switch between them on the calendar, or use the All Accounts view to see every transaction across all accounts in one place. Net worth updates automatically.

Set goals. Track progress.
Create savings goals with a target amount and date. Watch the progress bar grow as you save. Whether it is an emergency fund, a vacation, or a down payment — you will see exactly how close you are.

Budget by category.
Set monthly spending limits for any category you create. Real-time progress bars show what you have spent and what you have left — color coded so overspending catches your eye before it becomes a problem.

A personality for every mood.
Choose from 14 coaching modes — Professional, Mom Mode, Dad Mode, Spiritual Guide, CEO Mode, Coach, BFF, Drill Sergeant, Therapist, and more. Your daily budget messages match the tone that actually keeps you motivated. Switch anytime.

Family plan included.
Share your full calendar, accounts, and goals with a household member. One budget, two people, full transparency.

Beats to Budget.
Play lo-fi, hip hop, R&B, jazz, or gospel music while you budget. Five built-in vibes with the option to add your own YouTube stream.

Dark mode. Drag and drop. Recurring transactions. 12-month forecast strip. Everything you need in one dashboard.

Money Moves is built for people who are serious about where their money goes and where it is taking them. Not just this month. Seven years from now.
