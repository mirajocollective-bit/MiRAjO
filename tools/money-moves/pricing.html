Questions & Answers

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial? ▼
Yes — Stripe securely stores your card but won't charge it until your 30-day trial ends. Cancel any time before then and you won't pay a thing.
What's the difference between Individual and Family? ▼
Individual is a single-person account. Family lets up to 4 people share one household — same calendar, same accounts, same goals. Everyone sees changes in real time. Great for couples and households managing money together.
Can I switch from monthly to annual later? ▼
Yes. Go to Settings → Billing and click "Manage billing." You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch billing cycles anytime through your customer portal.
How do I log in? Do I need a password? ▼
No password needed. We send you a secure magic link every time you log in — just enter your email and click the link in your inbox. It's faster and more secure than a password.
Is my financial data secure? ▼
Yes. Money Moves does not connect to your bank accounts — you enter transactions manually, which means your bank credentials never touch our servers. Your data is encrypted and stored in Supabase (enterprise-grade Postgres). We never sell your data.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no questions asked.
