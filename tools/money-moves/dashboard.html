Getting Started
How do I set up my account? ▼
Start by adding at least one account — click + Add Account in the account bar at the top of the calendar. Give it a name (Checking, Savings, etc.), choose the account type, and enter your current balance. From there, add your first income and expense transactions and you're tracking.
How do I log income or an expense? ▼
Two ways:
  • Click + Add in the top nav bar
  • Click any day on the calendar to open the form pre-filled with that date
Choose Income, Expense, or Transfer, fill in the details, and hit Save.
What is a recurring transaction? ▼
Recurring transactions repeat automatically — weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, etc. Toggle Recurring when adding a transaction, set the frequency, and optionally an end date or count. They appear on every future occurrence in the calendar and feed directly into your 7-Year Forecast.
Can I add an emoji to a transaction? ▼
Yes. When adding or editing a transaction, click the emoji button next to the description field. Pick one from the shelf to personalize your entries — they appear right on the calendar pill so you can spot transactions at a glance.
How do I edit or delete a transaction? ▼
Click any transaction pill on the calendar to open it. You'll see options to edit the details or delete it entirely. If it's a recurring transaction, you can choose to delete just that occurrence or all future ones.
The Calendar
What do the colors and numbers on the calendar mean? ▼
  • Green pills = income transactions
  • Red pills = expense transactions
  • Grey pills = transfers between accounts
  • The small number in the bottom-right of each day cell = your running daily balance for the selected account
  • The today cell is highlighted with a green border and a filled green date circle
How do I navigate between months and years? ▼
  • Use the ← → arrows to move one month at a time
  • Use the « » arrows to jump a full year
  • Click the month/year heading to open the Jump to Date picker — choose any month and year directly
  • Click Today to snap back to the current month instantly
Can I move a transaction to a different date? ▼
Yes — drag and drop any transaction pill to a new day on the calendar. The date updates automatically. This works on desktop; on mobile, tap to edit and change the date manually.
How does the search work? ▼
Click the search bar (top right of the calendar header) and start typing. Results appear instantly — showing the transaction description, date, and amount. Click a result to jump to that date. Press Escape or the ✕ to clear.
What is the summary bar at the top? ▼
The summary bar shows four cards for the currently selected month and account: Account Balance, Income This Month, Expenses This Month, and Month Balance (income minus expenses). The bar on the right shows what percentage of your income was spent and what was saved.
What are the daily budget hints at the bottom? ▼
The hint bar at the bottom of the calendar rotates through personalized tips and reminders. Click the dots to cycle through them. Hints adapt based on your spending patterns and selected personality mode.
Forecast & Analytics
What is the 7-Year Forecast? ▼
Switch to Forecast in the view toggle (next to Calendar) to see a month-by-month projection of your cash flow for the next 7 years — based entirely on your recurring transactions. It shows projected income, expenses, net, and running balance. Use it to spot future shortfalls or surplus periods before they hit.
What is the 12-Month Balance Forecast strip? ▼
Click 📈 Forecast in the top nav to reveal a compact sparkline chart showing your projected balance over the next 12 months. Hover over it to see the projected balance for any future month. It stays collapsed by default — turn it on when you need a quick visual snapshot.
What does the Analytics sidebar show? ▼
The Analytics sidebar (click Analytics on the left edge of the dashboard) breaks down your spending and income by category for the selected month. Each category shows how much you've spent vs. your budget with a progress bar. Green = on track, yellow = approaching your limit, orange/red = over budget. Your net savings rate is shown at the bottom.
Budget, Goals & Debt
How do I set a budget for a category? ▼
In the Analytics sidebar, click any category's budget label to set a monthly limit for it. Once set, the progress bar tracks spending against your budget in real time throughout the month.
How do I track savings goals? ▼
In the right sidebar, find the Goals card. Click + Add Goal to create a savings goal with a target amount and optional target date. As you add income tagged to that goal, the progress bar updates. Use goals for emergency funds, vacations, down payments — anything you're building toward.
How does the Debt Payoff Calculator work? ▼
In the right sidebar, find the Debt Payoff card. Select a debt, enter the balance, interest rate, and minimum payment. The calculator shows your payoff date, total interest paid, and how much you save by adding extra monthly payments. It's the same math credit card companies don't want you to do.
Calculators
What is the Mortgage Calculator? ▼
Click the Mortgage tab in the calendar view toggle. Enter your home value, down payment, interest rate, and loan term. The calculator shows your estimated monthly payment broken down into principal, interest, taxes, insurance, PMI, and HOA. It also shows lifetime costs, an interest savings banner if you add extra payments, and a full amortization schedule. You can download or print a report directly from this tab.
What is the DTI Calculator? ▼
Click the DTI tab in the calendar view toggle. Enter your monthly gross income and all debt payments. The calculator shows your front-end ratio (housing costs vs. income) and back-end ratio (all debt vs. income) — color-coded green, yellow, orange, or red based on what lenders consider acceptable. Use this before applying for a mortgage, car loan, or any major credit.
What is the pop-up calculator? ▼
Click the calculator icon in the top-right nav bar to open a floating calculator you can drag anywhere on the screen. Use it to do quick math without leaving your dashboard — great for splitting bills or verifying totals before entering a transaction.
Accounts & Money
How do I switch between accounts? ▼
Click any account tab in the bar just below the nav. The calendar, summary cards, and running balance all update to show that account's transactions. Net Worth in the top-right of the account bar always reflects all accounts combined, regardless of which one is selected.
How do transfers work? ▼
Select Transfer when adding a transaction. Choose the source account and destination account. The amount is deducted from one and added to the other — it does not count as income or expense in your monthly summary, so your net balance stays accurate.
Can I reorder or customize the sidebar cards? ▼
Yes — on desktop, hover over any sidebar card to reveal the drag handle on the left. Drag cards up or down to reorder them however you prefer. Your order is saved automatically.
Family Plan
How do I add a family member? ▼
Go to Settings → Household. Enter your family member's email and send an invite. They'll receive a link to create their account and join your shared household. All accounts, transactions, and goals are shared across the household.
Can family members see and edit everything? ▼
Yes — all household members share the same view and can add or edit transactions. This is intentional. Money Moves is built around financial transparency within a household. If you need separate tracking, create separate accounts within the app for each person.
Personality Modes
What are personality modes? ▼
Personality modes change the tone of your daily budget coaching messages to match whatever voice keeps you motivated. Click "Change your mode" on the dashboard to switch anytime:
  • 💼 Professional — calm, clear, no pressure
  • 🤱 Mom Mode — nurturing but firm
  • 👨 Dad Mode — direct, no-nonsense
  • 🙏 Spiritual Guide — grounded and mindful
  • 🤼 Wrestling Mode — hype, intensity, trash-talk
  • 🏆 Coach Mode — game mindset, team energy
  • 👔 CEO Mode — boardroom strategy
  • 🫂 BFF Mode — real talk, supportive
  • 🎤 Hype Man — celebration energy
  • 👵 Grandma Mode — old-school wisdom and love
  • 🎖️ Drill Sergeant — military strict, zero excuses
  • 🛋️ Therapist Mode — reflective, emotionally intelligent
  • ✨ Gen Z Mode — very online, bestie energy
  • 😈 Villain Era — building an empire, no apologies
What's New
Dark mode — how do I turn it on? ▼
Click the 🌙 moon icon in the top nav bar to switch to dark mode. Click again (becomes ☀️) to return to light mode. Your preference is saved — it will stay in dark mode next time you open Money Moves.
What is the All Accounts view? ▼
Click the All Accounts tab at the left of the account bar to see a combined calendar showing income and expenses from every account in one view. Transfers are excluded since they cancel out. The summary bar shows your total balance across all accounts. Use this to see your full financial picture at a glance.
What are the Beats to Budget music options? ▼
Click Beats to Budget in the bottom-left corner to open the music player. Choose from five vibes: 🌙 Lo-Fi, 🔥 Hip Hop, ✨ R&B, 🎷 Jazz, or 🙏 Gospel. Click Customize to swap any track with your own YouTube URL or video ID. Settings save automatically.
How do the daily hints work? ▼
The hint bar at the top of the calendar rotates through tips and feature reminders every 5 seconds. Click any dot to jump to a specific hint. Tips cover things like drag-and-drop, recurring transactions, the forecast, and more — useful if you're still learning what Money Moves can do.
Your Account
How do I sign in? ▼
Go to the login page and sign in with Google, LinkedIn, or your email and password. Always use the same method you used when you first signed up — mixing methods creates separate accounts.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot your password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link in your inbox. Click it, set a new password, and you'll be taken directly to your dashboard. Note: this only works if you signed up with email/password — Google and LinkedIn accounts don't use a password.
How do I change my email or password? ▼
Go to Settings → Account to update your email or password. A confirmation email will be sent for any email changes.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
Go to Settings → Subscription to manage or cancel your plan. If you cancel, you'll retain access through the end of your current billing period. Your data is preserved — if you reactivate, everything will be right where you left it.
How do I log out? ▼
Click Log Out in the top-right of the nav bar. Your data is saved automatically — nothing is lost when you log out.
Contact Us
I have a question that's not answered here — how do I reach you? ▼
Email us directly at support@mirajoco.org and we'll get back to you. Include your account email and a brief description of what you're seeing so we can help you faster.
I found a bug or something isn't working right. ▼
We want to know. Email support@mirajoco.org with your account email and a description of what happened — including what you were doing when the issue occurred. Screenshots are always helpful if you can grab one.
I have a feature request or idea. ▼
We genuinely love hearing these. Send your idea to support@mirajoco.org — we read every email and the best ideas make it into future updates.
