Help & FAQ

How to get the most out of Money Moves

Getting Started
How do I add my first account? ▼
Click + Add Account in the account tab bar at the top. Give it a name (e.g. "Checking"), select the type, and enter your current balance. You can add as many accounts as you need — checking, savings, credit cards, or custom.
How do I log income or an expense? ▼
Two ways:
  • Click + Add in the top nav bar
  • Click any day on the calendar to open the add transaction form pre-filled with that date
Choose Income, Expense, or Transfer, fill in the details, and hit Save.
What is a recurring transaction? ▼
Recurring transactions repeat automatically — weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, etc. Toggle Recurring when adding a transaction, set the frequency, and optionally set an end date or count. These feed your 7-Year Forecast automatically.
The Calendar
What do the colors on the calendar mean? ▼
  • Green entries = income
  • Red entries = expenses
  • Grey entries = transfers between accounts
  • The small number in the bottom-right of each day = your running daily balance for that account
Can I move a transaction to a different date? ▼
Yes — just drag and drop any transaction pill to a new date on the calendar. The date updates automatically in your records.
How do I navigate between months? ▼
Use the ← → arrows in the top-right of the calendar to move back or forward one month at a time.
What is the Forecast view? ▼
Switch to Forecast (tab next to Calendar) to see a 7-year month-by-month projection of your cash flow based on your recurring transactions. It's a powerful planning tool — great for spotting future shortfalls or surplus months before they happen.
Accounts & Money
How do I switch between accounts on the calendar? ▼
Click any account tab in the bar below the nav. The calendar, summary cards, and balance will all update to show that account's transactions. Net Worth (top-right of the account bar) always reflects all accounts combined.
What is Net Worth? ▼
Net Worth is the total of all your account balances combined. It's always visible in the top-right of the account bar regardless of which account you're viewing.
How do transfers work? ▼
Select Transfer when adding a transaction. Choose the source account and destination account. The amount is deducted from one and added to the other — it won't count as income or expense in your monthly summary.
Family Plan
How do I add a family member? ▼
Go to Settings → Household. Enter your family member's email and send an invite. They'll receive a link to create their account and join your shared household. All accounts, transactions, and goals are shared across the household.
Can family members see everything? ▼
Yes — all household members share the same view. This is intentional. Money Moves is designed for financial transparency within a household. Each member can add and edit transactions.
Personality Modes
What are personality modes? ▼
Personality modes change the tone of your budget coaching messages. Choose the voice that motivates you:
  • 🤱 Mom Mode — nurturing but firm
  • 💼 Dad Mode — direct, no-nonsense
  • 🙏 Spiritual Guide — grounded and mindful
  • 🤼 Wrestling Mode — hyped, intense, trash-talk motivation
Switch anytime in Settings.
