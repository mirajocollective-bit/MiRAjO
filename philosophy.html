The Hard Questions

Truth has always been in the asking.

We are all self-made. Not just in the millionaire sense ‚Äî but in the way we fail too. Every result in your life is a seed you planted. Your relationships, your finances, your health, your peace ‚Äî all of it is a harvest. If you don't like what you're reaping, the question isn't who did this to you. The question is: what have you been sowing?
Your time and your money are seeds. Where they go determines what grows. Good ground multiplies. Bad ground consumes. You can work hard in the wrong soil your entire life and still have nothing to show for it. Stop pouring into environments, relationships, and systems that cannot hold what you are planting. Assess your ground before you assess your effort.
There are three levels. Level 1: Crawl and Walk. Level 2: Run. Level 3: Fly. Most people live and die at Level 1 ‚Äî not because they couldn't reach Level 3, but because they never identified which level they were on. You cannot run until you have finished learning to walk. And most people assign "fly" to the afterlife or the next generation. It doesn't have to be that way. But you have to be honest about where you actually are right now.
This is not new. It is the oldest operating law in existence ‚Äî pre-dating every philosophy, every religion, every financial system. Seedtime and Harvest governs everything: your body, your relationships, your business, your legacy. The reason most people miss it is because they are always reacting to the harvest without ever examining the seed. Once you learn this law, you cannot unsee it.
Every conversation is a seed. Every dollar is a seed. Every hour of silence or noise is a seed. This is bigger than karma ‚Äî because karma is reactive. Seedtime and Harvest is intentional. You choose the seed. You choose the ground. You choose the season. Karma happens to you. Seedtime and Harvest is something you do. The question is whether you are doing it consciously or by default.
