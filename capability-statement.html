What Sets MiRAjO Collective Apart
Built From Real Operations
MiRAjO is not a consulting firm that studies entrepreneurship. Our founder built three companies generating $9.5M+ in combined revenue — 20 trucks running nationwide in logistics, six-figure construction contracts with DR Horton and Ryland Homes, and a licensed real estate brokerage. 50+ employees hired. Every program is tested in the real world first.
Published Frameworks
Two published books — The Pain Principle and LeadLoud — form the curriculum foundation. Programs are not loosely assembled content. They are structured frameworks with clear stages, measurable outcomes, and completion paths.
Dual-County Certified Provider
DM/DWBE certified in both Hillsborough and Pinellas counties. Licensed educational provider in the City of St. Petersburg. Qualified for minority set-aside, woman-owned, and disadvantaged business contracts at the local, state, and federal levels.
