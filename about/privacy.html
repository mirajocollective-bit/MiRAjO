Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026 · MiRAjO Collective LLC · Florida, USA

MiRAjO Collective LLC ("MiRAjO," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect across all of our products and services, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

This policy applies to:

  • www.mirajoco.org — our main website
  • 25 Days & 25 Nights — online course
  • Couples in Entrepreneurship (CIE) — couples course and diagnostic program
  • Money Moves by MiRAjO — budget planning web app and Android application
  • Coaching Services — individual and group coaching programs
  • Free Tools — Alignment Audit, Couples Scorecard, and similar assessments
  • Newsletter and waitlists — email communications you opt into

By using any of these services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address submitted through any form, enrollment, or inquiry
  • Account credentials when you create a login for any of our programs or tools
  • Assessment and diagnostic responses (Alignment Audit, Couples Scorecard, CIE Diagnostic)
  • Budget entries, income and expense categories, calendar events, and financial goals entered in Money Moves
  • Couples pairing information entered during CIE enrollment
  • Communications you send us by email or through contact forms

Payment information: When you purchase a course, subscription, or coaching program, payment is processed securely by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

Automatically collected data: When you visit our website or use our apps, we may collect browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and referring URLs through standard server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process payments and fulfill your enrollment or subscription
  • To create and maintain your account and program access
  • To deliver course content, track progress, and issue certificates
  • To store and sync your Money Moves budget data across devices
  • To generate your diagnostic results and profile reports (CIE, Alignment Audit, Couples Scorecard)
  • To send transactional emails — receipts, login confirmations, course updates
  • To send marketing emails you have opted into — you may unsubscribe at any time
  • To respond to coaching inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website, tools, and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Money Moves — Android App

Money Moves by MiRAjO is available as a web application and as an Android app on the Google Play Store. Both use the same account system and data storage. When you use the Android app:

  • We collect the same account and financial planning data described in Section 1
  • The app does not access your device contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • The app requires an internet connection to sync your data with your account
  • Your financial planning data is never sold or shared with third parties, and is never used for advertising purposes
  • You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at info@mirajoco.org

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers to operate our business. These providers may process your data on our behalf for purposes including payment processing, database storage, email delivery, website hosting, and bot protection. All providers are contractually required to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for site functionality, including login sessions and payment processing.
  • Preference cookies — remember your choices, such as dismissing banners.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain features including course access and checkout.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@mirajoco.org.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
