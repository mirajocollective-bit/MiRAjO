Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026 · MiRAjO Collective LLC · Florida, USA

MiRAjO Collective LLC ("MiRAjO," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using www.mirajoco.org, the Money Moves by MiRAjO web app and Android application, and any associated services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — name, email address, and any other details you submit through forms, course enrollment, or coaching inquiries.
  • Payment information — when you make a purchase or subscribe to a plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.
  • Account information — when you create an account, we store your email and profile data in our secure database (Supabase). For course users, this includes course progress. For Money Moves users, this includes your subscription plan and account preferences.
  • Financial planning data (Money Moves) — if you use Money Moves by MiRAjO, we store the budget entries, income and expense categories, calendar events, and financial goals you create within the app. This data is tied to your account and stored securely in Supabase. We do not share this data with third parties or use it for advertising purposes.
  • Communications — if you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence.
  • Automatically collected data — browser type, device, IP address, pages visited, and referring URLs, collected via cookies and server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process payments and fulfill course or program enrollment
  • To provide and maintain your account access
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, login links, course updates)
  • To send marketing emails you have opted into — you may unsubscribe at any time
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Money Moves by MiRAjO — Android App

Money Moves by MiRAjO is available as a web application at mirajoco.org/tools/money-moves and as an Android application on the Google Play Store. Both platforms use the same account system and data storage. When you use the Android app:

  • We collect the same account and financial planning data described in Section 1.
  • The app does not access your device contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
  • The app requires an internet connection to sync your data with your account.
  • You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting info@mirajoco.org.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — database and user authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Resend — email delivery (resend.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Eventbrite — event listings and registration (eventbrite.com/privacypolicy)
  • Brilliant Directory — business directory at directory.mirajoco.org
  • Vercel — website hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing the data they process.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for site functionality, including authentication (login sessions) and payment processing via Stripe.
  • Preference cookies — remember your choices, such as dismissing banners.
  • Third-party cookies — set by services such as Stripe, Supabase, and Eventbrite for their own functional purposes.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain features, including course access and checkout.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@mirajoco.org.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
