The Framework
Your Day, Structured
This schedule places all 10 Non-Negotiables into a logical flow. Times are a starting template — adjust to your life. What matters is that every Non-Negotiable happens. Move the time. Never skip the practice.
Time Activity Non-Negotiable
☀ Morning Block
5:30 – 6:00
Wake up. Glass of water. Dress for how you want to feel.
6:00 – 6:30
Morning Meditation — Set intention. Sit in silence. Let the day come to you.
#4 Morning Meditation
6:30 – 7:00
Breakfast — Protein + fiber. Hydrate. Fuel for movement.
#7 Nutrition

Cardio Session 1 — Walk, bike, elliptical, water aerobics. 30 minutes.

Plank Set 1 — 3 planks × 1 min each. Rest 30 sec between. Hydrate after.

#1 Cardio 3×30

#2 Planks

Strength Training — 15–20 min minimum. Legs, arms, or full body. Use what you have.

🌙 Evening Block
6:00 – 6:30
Dinner — Recovery meal. Protein, veg, complex carbs. Eat with presence.
#7 Nutrition

#2 Planks

Evening Meditation — Release the day. Release the people. Return to yourself.
#5 Evening Meditation
9:00 – 9:15
Journaling — Check-in questions. Today's prompt. Honest reflection.

Wind down. Dark room. No screens. Signal your body it is time to repair.

10:00 PM
Lights out. 7–9 hours of sleep in a quiet, dark space.

Non-Negotiable #8 — No alcohol or recreational drugs applies all day, every day. This is your baseline, not a reminder.
