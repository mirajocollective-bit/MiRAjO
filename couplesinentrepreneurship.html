The four domains

Every area that matters when you build together.

📈

Business

You can love the same business and have completely different visions for where it is going. The scenarios in this domain surface that before it costs you: how decisions get made, who is carrying what, and whether both of you feel like you have a real seat at the table.

  • Do you both see the same long-term vision?
  • How does one partner's strength become the other's silence?
  • When one person does more, does the other know?
🤝

Relationship

Intimacy, connection, and shared values are not soft topics. They are the foundation of everything. This domain looks at what happens to the relationship when the business gets louder and whether both partners are even aware of it.

  • Does the business feel more important than the relationship?
  • Are you filling in gaps about what your partner believes?
  • When did you stop choosing each other first?
💰

Finances

The tension is rarely about the numbers. It is about what the numbers mean to each person. This domain reads how both partners think about money, what they do when things are tight, and what they do when there is more than expected.

  • What happens when there is surplus and no agreement on how to use it?
  • Who holds the financial anxiety and who holds the financial decisions?
  • What does financial security actually mean to each of you?
🏡

Family

Home life does not pause for the business. When the load is uneven, the resentment builds quietly and then loudly. This domain names it, including the dynamics that come with blended families, young children, and different ideas about what home is supposed to feel like.

  • Who is carrying the home and is that the agreement you both agreed to?
  • How does your family structure shape what you each need from this business?
  • What are you each protecting your kids from without telling the other?
