The four domains

Every area that matters when you build together.

📈

Business

You can love the same business and have completely different visions for where it is going. The Diagnostic surfaces that before it costs you.

  • Where do you see the business in three years?
  • How are decisions made and do you feel heard?
  • Is your current role working for you?
🤝

Relationship

Intimacy, connection, and faith are not soft topics. They are the foundation. When the business is hard, this is what holds or breaks.

  • How has intimacy shifted since you started building?
  • Do you have a shared faith or spiritual practice?
  • Do you feel like partners first or co-founders first?
💰

Money

Money is the most common source of tension in business partnerships. Not because of the numbers, but because of what the numbers mean to each person.

  • Who manages the finances and is that working?
  • How much do you reinvest versus take home?
  • What does financial security actually mean to you?
🏡

Family

Home life does not pause for the business. When it is uneven, the resentment builds quietly and then loudly. This domain names it.

  • How are home responsibilities divided?
  • Does the business take too much from family time?
  • Where is the friction in your daily home life?
