How it works

Four steps. Built around who you actually are.

1

Your Individual Profile

Each partner builds a private profile covering your background, family structure, business stage, financial picture, and what you are personally hoping to get out of this. Five minutes. Yours alone. Your partner does theirs separately.

2

The Partnership Diagnostic

Each partner reads real scenarios across Business, Finances, Family, and Relationship. Not a rating scale. Actual situations that happen in couples-run businesses. You choose how you see them. Your partner sees nothing until the report is ready.

3

The Partnership Report

Once both of you submit, the report generates automatically. It shows each partner's perspective side by side across every domain, where you see things the same and where you do not. You open it together. That conversation is the beginning of the work.

4

Your Joint Course Path

The modules that unlock are driven by your report. Strong in finances, not in relationship? You skip what you do not need and go deep where it matters. The course works for your specific gaps, not a template built for someone else.

The four domains

Every area that matters when you build together.

📈

Business

How decisions get made, who is carrying what, and whether both of you feel like you have a real seat at the table. You can love the same business and have completely different visions for where it is going.

🤝

Relationship

What happens to the relationship when the business gets louder. Intimacy, connection, and shared values are not soft topics. They are the foundation of everything else.

💰

Finances

The tension is rarely about the numbers. It is about what the numbers mean to each person. This domain reads how both partners think about money and what they do when things shift.

🏡

Family

Home life does not pause for the business. When the load is uneven, resentment builds quietly and then loudly. This domain names what neither partner wants to say out loud.
