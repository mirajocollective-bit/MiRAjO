About the Program

Leadership that starts from the inside out.

Her Vitality is a health-centered leadership program built for women who lead — and who are ready to lead themselves first. Across five powerful modules, participants address the mental, physical, financial, and personal dimensions of leadership that are rarely talked about in traditional programs.

All modules require full participation including Round Table Discussions and written feedback — because real growth happens in the room, not from a workbook.

Proudly sponsored by Florida Blue and presented by Tampa Business Club in partnership with MiRAjO Collective.

The Five Modules

Every dimension of your vitality. Covered.

Module 01

Mental Health in Leadership

Building mental resilience and healthy emotional management in leadership roles.

  • Breaking free from perfectionism
  • Emotional expression and boundaries
  • Mindfulness practices for leaders
  • Personalized self-care planning
Module 02

Physical Health as Foundation

Recognizing the direct connection between physical wellness and leadership effectiveness.

  • Moving beyond the "tough it out" mentality
  • Nutrition and exercise routines that work
  • Body image and self-perception in leadership
  • Physical wellness schedule development
Module 03

Financial Health

Moving from financial dependence or impulsive spending toward lasting financial confidence.

  • Money mindset and financial identity
  • Budgeting and building independence
  • Balancing generosity with self-care
  • Digital budget planning tools
Module 04

Life Balance

Finding a fulfilling balance between professional ambitions and personal life — without guilt.

  • Relationship dynamics and boundaries
  • Building a personal support system
  • Emotional intelligence in leadership
  • Goal-setting across all life areas
Module 05

Legacy & Long-Term Success

Creating a legacy that values balance, emotional intelligence, and well-being above all.

  • Redefining what success means for you
  • Mentorship opportunities and giving back
  • Addressing systemic challenges for women
  • Sustainability planning for the long game
Case Studies

Research that makes it real.

"Each participant conducts original research examining how mental, physical, financial, and life-balance health have affected the career longevity of women leaders historically — then presents their findings to the group."
