Who Miranda is

20 years of seeds

Miranda Johnson didn't arrive here through a smooth path. She grew up navigating environments that required resilience before she even knew the word. She built businesses, lost businesses, and rebuilt. Not because it was easy, but because stopping wasn't an option she was willing to take.

She's the author of The Pain Principle and LeadLoud: 11 Commandments of the Next-Gen Leader. A speaker. A community builder. One of Tampa Bay's most trusted voices in entrepreneurship and leadership development.

Her framework, Seedtime and Harvest, is built on a foundational truth: everything you've planted, every sacrifice, every hard season, every thing you thought was lost, it has a harvest. The work is stopping the avoidance and starting the tending.

Her anchor: "Inside of me is Him." That's not a tagline. That's the foundation she coaches from. It's the thing people feel in the room.
