The Creator

Built by someone who actually built something

"Peace is the destination. Everything we build is designed to get you there."

Miranda Johnson spent seven years in construction and fifteen years building two trucking companies before she turned her focus to education and technology. She is the founder of MiRAjO Collective, author of The Pain Principle and LeadLoud, and a speaker and educator who teaches entrepreneurship, leadership, and financial strategy to real people doing real things.

MiRAjO was built because real-world experience should not be locked behind a degree or a network you were never invited into.
